  • The client has no right to verbally or physically assault any of the employees of the company, or else he/she will be subjected to legal liability.
4. Terms of postponement
  • In case the customer requested to postpone the cleaning date within (24) forty-eight hours of the service time and with a working time of not more than (1-7 hours), the amount of (5) five KDs is paid. For every working hour.
  • In case the customer requested to postpone the cleaning date within (24) forty-eight hours of the service time and with a working time of (8) eight hours and more, the amount of 70 KDs only, is collected.
  • The customer has the right to postpone any service visit for not more than 30 days of the original date of the visit, and in case of more than 30 days the visit will be considered cancelled and the amount of the visiting fees is considered a total right of the company and not refundable.
5. Cancellation terms
  • In case that the customer cancels the service in (48) forty-eight hours in advance, the amount will be kept in the customer's account with the company for a period of not more than (6) six months to be used later in the services provided by the company.
  • In case the customer requests that the cleaning date be cancelled within (48) forty-eight hours of the service delivery time of no more than (1-7) hours, the full amount of the service will be collected.
  • If the duration of the work is a full day (or more than (8) eight hours) the value of the first day will be collected in addition to the amount of only (50) fifty KDs, that is for every extra day.
